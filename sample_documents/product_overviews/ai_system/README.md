# AI System

## Overview
The <b>AI System</b> is the foundational layer for Artificial Intelligence (AI) and Machine Learning (ML) capabilities available in the Technology Portal (TP).

With the </b>AI System</b>, you can quickly classify and extract information from documents with unstructured or semi-structured content, provide translation services, or host custom models in your workflows.

And the list is growing on AI capabilities!

We provide the documentation, code samples, helper libraries, and developer tools you need on the AI journey, and you provide your imagination.

Let’s build something amazing together!
